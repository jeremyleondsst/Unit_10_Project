Goku's History

Goku is a low-class warrior born into the Saiyajin race; they were sent to extinction by Freeza- a powerful tyrant during his prime. They were originally located in planet Vegeta, ruled by King Vegeta, however, due to their extinction they're now residing on planet Earth.

Goku, throughout his earlier lifetime, has achieved many accomplishments many of the earthlings couldn't grasp. He was strong compared to many, and was adopted by his grandfather Gohan, that's what he codenames himself.

Goku, throughout his journey of martial arts, has embarked on the style of the turtle hermit- taught by his first master Muten-Roshi, who also showed Goku the KameHameHa, which means the hawaiin king.

Eventually, as Goku has gained strength, and faught off the evil King Piccolo, who was also an alien, he has trancended that of the normal saiyans. During his fight with Freeza, he mastered the Super Saiyan transformation, and through his lifetime with many duels has unlocked Super Saiyan 2,3, God, Blue, and most recently, an individual form "Ultra Instinct".

Goku has successfully trancended the barrier between gods and mortals, as with the new Ultra Instinct form, he has mastered self-movement. His body now reacts by itself, not wasting precious miliseconds whereas you would by having to think about your opponents moves. Many gods are fearful of Goku's potential in the Tournament of Power, which decides the fate of 8 universes, and the only one who has stopped him, and beats Goku easily is Jiren the Gray, the most powerful warrior of Universe 11, who also has Ultra Instinct.
